Ingredients

Everything you need for this recipe

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Instructions

Step-by-step cooking guide

  1. 1Heat vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
  2. 2Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  3. 3Add harder vegetables first (carrots, broccoli) and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. 4Add softer vegetables (bell peppers) and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. 5Mix soy sauce with cornstarch and add to the pan.
  6. 6Drizzle with sesame oil and toss everything together.
  7. 7Garnish with green onions and serve immediately over rice.
